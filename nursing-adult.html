The AG-ACNP curriculum requires five consecutive quarters of full-time study and completion of a minimum of 38 credit hours. Students interested in completing the AG-ACNP certification as part of the DNP program should meet with a DNP advisor to create an individual program of study.

Fall I
NURS 5400 Advanced Inpatient Pharmacology (2)
NURS 5401: Diagnostics and Procedures (4)
Total Credits: 6
Winter
NURS 5402: Acute Care I - Management of Chronic Conditions in the Acute Care Setting (5)
NURS 5403: Acute Care Practicum I - Management of Chronic Conditions during Acute Alterations in Health (3)
Total Credits: 8
Spring
NURS 5404: Acute Care II - Management of Acute and Complex Conditions (5)
NURS 5405: Acute Care Practicum II - Management of Acute Conditions during Altered States of Health (3)
Total Credits: 8
Summer
NURS 5406: Applied Critical Care Pharmacology (2)
NURS 5407: Acute Care III - Advance Practice Critical Care (5)
NURS 5408: Acute Care Practicum III - Management of Critical or Unstable Conditions during Altered States of Health (3)
Total Credits: 10
Fall II
NURS 5409: Acute Care Practicum IV - Specialty Focus (6)
Total Credits: 6

* A typical program of study is subject to change based on faculty and other resource availability.&nbsp;Current students should consult with their advisor and official SU graduate catalog for program requirements.
