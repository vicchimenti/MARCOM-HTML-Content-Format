III. Major Requirements

25 credits, including:
Course Number Course Title Credits
FINC 3420 Intermediate Corporate Finance 5

FINC Electives* 10
*Choose from upper-division finance courses.
Note: Students are advised to take the two finance major electives from FINC 4410, 4450, 4460, or 4480 if they plan to pursue a career in corporate finance; or from FINC 4450, 4460, 4510, or 4520 if they plan to pursue a career in investments. Internships and independent studies may not satisfy a major requirement.
