Thank you for your interest in Housing & Residence Life.

The Department of Housing & Residence Life (HRL) employs a broad range of positions from Resident Assistants (RAs) to learning community mentors and Graduate Assistants (GAs). We also have full-time positions including Resident Directors (RDs) and Assistant Resident Directors (ARDs).

Housing & Residence Life also offers leadership development opportunities to on campus residents through the Residence Hall Association and Hall Council. If you are interested in student leadership roles, check out the Residence Hall Association webpage.

Professional Careers

Housing & Residence Life creates and maintains an environment that is open to and respectful of individuals across the spectrum of human differences and distinctions. The pace is fast and the energy is high. Through partnerships with others we provide quality programs, services and facilities for those we serve in a caring, responsible and cost-effective manner.

There are a variety of professional positions within Housing & Residence Life. These positions range from live-in residential programs professionals to central office positions, and many others. All of our permanent staff positions are posted through the Seattle University Jobs Site.
