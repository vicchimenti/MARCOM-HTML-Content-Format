To earn a minor in finance, students must complete four prescribed finance courses and two prescribed business classes beyond the non-business prerequisite courses in mathematics. The mathematics and the economics courses can fulfill university core requirements.

Courses used toward a minor in finance are subject to the same grade minimums as those in a finance major. See policy for minors for more information.
