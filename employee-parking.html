A - Permit for Faculty/Staff

This permit is for Faculty & Staff members who are commuting to SU during the day. Valid from 6:00am to Midnight in these locations:

  • Broadway Garage (Broadway & E Columbia St)
  • 13th and E Cherry St Lot (13th & E Cherry)
  • Redhawk Complex Lot (14th & E Jefferson St)
  • Library Lot (10th & E Columbia St)
  • Murphy Garage (11th & E James Way)
  • Visitor Lot (12th Ave & E Marion St.) valid only after 3pm and all day on weekends

This permit may be purchased online through the Permit Store

** Beginning Summer Semester 2018/Summer Quarter 2018 budget numbers may not be used to purchase employee permits. Permits must be purchased by the employee directly and payment method of payroll deduction, Visa, or Mastercard.  For questions or clarification, please contact Payroll at paroll@seattleu.edu or Public Safety at parkingservices@seattleu.edu

Motorcycle Permit

Motorcycle parking permits are issued for a full calendar year (12 months from the date of purchase). Motorcycles are allowed to park in any motorcycle designated area on campus. Motorcycles are not authorized to park in a parking space intended for an automobile.

This permit may only be purchased at the Public Safety front desk (USVC 102) after your motorcycle has been registered online. To register your motorcycle, follow the purchasing your permit steps located here and select "Vehicle Registration" instead of a permit or ORCA card.

1 - 3 Day Intermittent Pass

This permit is for persons whom are commuting to school during the day. Parking is valid in these locations:

  • Broadway Garage (Broadway & E Columbia St) 
  • 13th and E Cherry St Lot (13th & E Cherry) 
  • Redhawk Complex Lot (14th & E Jefferson St) 
  • Murphy Garage (11th & E James Way) 
  • Visitor Lot (12th Ave & E Marion St.) valid only after 3pm and all day on weekends

The pass must be pre-ordered through the Permit Store.
