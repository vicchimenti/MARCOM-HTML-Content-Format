A Graduate Certificate in Software Engineering will provide you with knowledge and skills needed for design, analysis, development, and management of large scale software systems. This is a part-time program well suited to working professionals who want to enhance their career. The program consists of three required courses and one elective.

To enroll, you must have a bachelor's degree in computer science or a related discipline. Upon successful completion of the certificate program, you may apply the courses toward the Master of Science in Computer Science degree.
