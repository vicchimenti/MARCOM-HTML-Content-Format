Day Locker Usage 

There are a number of day use lockers available for no-charge in each locker room. The Fitness Center Member Services Desk issues complementary rental combo locks with an I.D. or locks can be purchased at the Desk for $6. All locks on a day use locker must be returned by closing and are subject to being cut off at the end of the day upon facility closure and failure to return.

Renting a Locker 

We currently have lockers available to be rented in all of our locker rooms. If you would like to rent one, please stop by the Member Services so we may assist you.

Renewals  

We will do our best to send an email reminder to renew your locker up to two weeks in advance of your expiration however please note it is your responsibility to renew or clear out your locker prior to or on the expiration date. Because of the high demand for lockers, expiring lockers must be either renewed or cleaned out by the expiration date so that vacancies can be rented out to those on our waitlist.

Failure to renew or clear out an expired locker will result in the lock being cut, items being stored in lost and found, and after 15 days items will be donated.
