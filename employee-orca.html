  • Orca Transit Passes subsidized at 72%, are available to benefits eligible employees who do not currently have a parking pass. 
  • Seattle University provides an annual Transit Pass for benefits eligible employees on Metro, Kitsap, Community and Everett Transit, and Sound Transit.
  • With a Seattle University employee transit pass, it provides up to $100 per month toward Community Transit and Metro Van Pools.
  • Washington State Ferry (WSF) product must be added to your SU ORCA Pass and are purchased monthly by payroll deduction.
  • SU's Employee Transportation Coordinator (ETC) is Whitney Wedge, and can be reached by calling 206-296-5992, or by email: transitpass@seattleu.edu .
