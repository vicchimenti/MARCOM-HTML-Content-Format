D-Permit: Day Commuter

This permit is for students living off-campus who are commuting to school during the day.

  • Broadway Garage (Broadway & E Columbia St) 6:00am to Midnight
  • 13th and E Cherry St & Lot (13th & E Cherry) 6:00am to Midnight
  • Redhawk Center (14th & E Jefferson) 6:00am to Midnight
  • Murphy Garage (11th Ave & E James Way), 6:00am to Midnight
  • Visitor Lot (12th Ave & E Marion St) only after 3:00pm and all day on weekends

This permit may be purchased online through The Permit Store

R-Permit: On-Campus Resident

This permit is only for students who are on-campus residents. Valid for 24 Hours in these locations:

  • 13th and E Cherry St Lot (13th & E Cherry) all day/night
  • Redhawk Center (14th & E Jefferson) all day/night
  • Murphy Garage (11th Ave & E James Way) all day/night
  • Visitor Lot (12th & E Marion St) only after 3:00pm and all day on weekends

This permit may be purchased online through The Permit Store

Motorcycle Permit

Motorcycle parking permits are issued for a full calendar year (12 months from the date of purchase). Motorcycles are allowed to park in any motorcycle designated area on campus. Motorcycles are not authorized to park in a parking space intended for an automobile.

This permit may only be purchased at the Public Safety front desk (USVC 102) after your motorcycle has been registered online. Register your motorcycle through iParq, enter your SU ID number and your SU e-mail and add "Vehicle Registration" and continue with the process.
